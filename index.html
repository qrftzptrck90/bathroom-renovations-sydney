Bathroom Renovations in Sydney: Everything You Need to Know

By Iqra Fitzpatrick · March 25, 2026 · 5 min read

If you've been thinking about upgrading your home, there's no better place to start than the bathroom. Whether you're dealing with outdated tiles, a cramped layout, or fixtures that have seen better days, a well-planned renovation can completely transform your space — and add serious value to your property.

For Sydney homeowners, the good news is that the city has a thriving renovation industry with skilled tradespeople, quality suppliers, and a strong culture of home improvement. When it comes to bathroom renovations in Sydney, the options are virtually endless — whether you're after a budget-friendly refresh or a full luxury overhaul.

Cosmetic Refresh vs Full Renovation

One of the first decisions you'll need to make is how far you want to go. A cosmetic update — swapping out tapware, regrouting tiles, updating the vanity mirror and lighting — can make a surprisingly big difference for a relatively modest outlay. You don't always need to gut the whole room to achieve a fresh, modern look.

A full structural renovation, on the other hand, involves replumbing, retiling, and potentially reconfiguring the entire layout. This is the right approach if your bathroom is poorly laid out, has water damage, or simply doesn't meet the needs of your household. While it requires a bigger investment, the results are transformational.

Popular Design Styles in Sydney

Sydney homeowners tend to favour clean, contemporary designs that reflect the city's coastal lifestyle. Large format tiles, frameless glass shower screens, freestanding bathtubs, and wall-hung vanities are all extremely popular right now. Neutral palettes — think white, warm grey, and soft taupe — continue to dominate, offering a timeless look that won't date quickly.

For those who want something with a bit more personality, earthy tones, textured tiles, and natural stone accents are having a real moment. Brushed brass and matte black tapware have also replaced chrome as the hardware finish of choice in many Sydney renovations, adding a touch of sophistication to even simple designs.

Smart Storage Solutions

Storage is one of the biggest pain points in Australian bathrooms, particularly in apartments and older homes where space is limited. Smart storage planning can make a huge difference to how functional your bathroom feels day-to-day. Recessed wall niches in the shower, built-in vanity drawers, mirror cabinets, and floating shelves are all excellent ways to maximise space without cluttering the room.

Custom vanities are increasingly popular in Sydney renovations for exactly this reason — they allow you to design storage around your specific needs, making use of every available centimetre.

What Does a Bathroom Renovation Cost in Sydney?

Costs vary significantly depending on the size of the bathroom, the quality of materials, and the scope of the work involved. As a rough guide, a basic renovation for a small bathroom typically starts from around $10,000–$15,000, while a mid-range renovation for a standard-sized bathroom generally falls in the $15,000–$25,000 range. A high-end master ensuite with premium finishes can cost $30,000 or more.

It's always worth getting at least two or three quotes from reputable local builders before committing. Make sure each quote covers the same scope of work so you're comparing apples with apples.

Choosing the Right Builder

The most important factor in any successful renovation is choosing the right tradesperson. Look for a licensed builder with strong experience in bathroom renovations specifically, a solid portfolio of completed projects, and genuine positive reviews from past clients. A good builder will help you navigate design decisions, keep the project on schedule, and deliver a finished result that stands the test of time.

Don't be afraid to ask questions upfront — about timelines, what's included in the quote, how variations are handled, and what warranties are provided on the work. A reputable builder will welcome the conversation.

Final Thoughts

A bathroom renovation is one of the most rewarding investments you can make as a Sydney homeowner. Done well, it improves your daily quality of life, modernises your home, and adds genuine value at resale. Whether you're planning a simple refresh or a complete transformation, taking the time to plan properly and work with the right professionals makes all the difference.

Ready to get started? Explore your options for bathroom renovations in Sydney and take the first step toward the bathroom you've always wanted.
